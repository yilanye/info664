[Oscar Winners of 2011-2020]

By: [Yilan Ye]

  • Launch Project
  • View on Github
[In this project, I used the data of The Oscar Award, 1927 - 2020, and Movie Database (IMDB Alternative) to make visualizations of Oscar winners of 2011-2020. Number of Oscars and IMDB rating are both good indicators of the qualification of a film, and therefore the visualizations I created could give the overall ranking of these Oscar-Winning Films by the Number of Oscars and IMDB rating.]
